Advanced Water Quality Testing

Our rigorous 9-step testing process ensures the highest quality mineral water and beverages

Our Quality Assurance Process

At our state-of-the-art facility, we employ a comprehensive 9-step testing methodology to ensure that every drop of water meets the highest standards of purity and quality. Our advanced testing procedures guarantee that our products are not only safe but also deliver optimal taste and mineral content.

1

Chlorination

Our chlorination process effectively eliminates harmful bacteria and pathogens, ensuring the water is safe for consumption while maintaining its natural properties.

2

Micro-filtration

Using advanced micro-filtration technology, we remove microscopic particles and impurities down to 0.1 microns, preserving the water's clarity and purity.

3

Sand Vessels Filtration

Our multi-layer sand filtration system effectively removes suspended solids and organic matter, improving water clarity and taste.

4

Carbon Vessel Filtration

Activated carbon filtration removes chlorine, organic compounds, and unwanted odors, enhancing the water's taste profile and purity.

5

Reverse Osmosis

Our state-of-the-art reverse osmosis system removes dissolved solids, contaminants, and impurities at the molecular level, ensuring exceptional purity.

6

Mineral Dosing

We carefully reintroduce essential minerals in precise amounts to create the perfect mineral balance for optimal taste and health benefits.

7

Ultra Filtration

Ultra filtration technology removes viruses, bacteria, and the finest particles while preserving beneficial minerals for a superior product.

8

PH Level Balancing

We precisely adjust the pH levels to create a perfectly balanced water that is neither too acidic nor too alkaline for optimal taste and health.

9

Ionization

Our ionization process enhances the water's molecular structure, improving hydration efficiency and creating a smoother, more refreshing taste.
