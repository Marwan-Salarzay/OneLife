Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Beverage World products?

Beverage World products are available at major grocery stores, convenience stores, and online retailers nationwide. You can also purchase directly from our website with home delivery options.

Are your beverages gluten-free?

Yes, all Beverage World products are gluten-free. We take great care to ensure our beverages are safe for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship to the United States and Canada. We're working on expanding our shipping options to more countries. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we begin shipping to your country.

Are your bottles recyclable?

Yes, all our packaging is 100% recyclable. We're committed to sustainability and are constantly working to reduce our environmental footprint. We also use recycled materials in our packaging whenever possible.
